Paste your site URL and email, and we will send you a report with any broken links we find.

This is useful before a launch, after content changes, or as a quick regular check.

## Request fields

- Website URL
- Use a full URL, like `https://docs.example.com`.
- Email
- We will send your report to this email address.

## Advanced options

- Check external links: also checks links that go to other websites.
- Check section links: checks links that jump to a section on a page, like `#pricing`.
- Max pages to scan (optional): leave blank to scan as many pages as we find.
