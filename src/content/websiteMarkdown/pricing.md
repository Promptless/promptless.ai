All plans include a 14-day free trial.

Pricing scales with the size and complexity of your docs site.

Open-source, non-commercial project? [Learn about our OSS program](https://promptless.ai/docs/getting-started/promptless-oss).

## Startup

For teams with small docs sites and standard integration needs.

- Price: $500/mo
- Usage: Up to 200 Pages*
- 14 day free trial
- Slack + GitHub integrations
- Unlimited contributors
- Unlimited triggers
- Unlimited documentation updates
- Slack Connect support channel
- CTA: Sign up

## Growth

For teams with larger docs sites and broader integrations.

### Available bundles

- 200-500 Pages: $500-$1,000/mo
- 500-1,000 Pages: $1,000-$1,500/mo
- 1,000-2,000 Pages: $1,500-$2,000/mo
- 2,000-5,000 Pages: $2,000-$4,000/mo

### Everything in Startup, plus

- Expanded integrations (Linear, Jira, Confluence, Notion, and more)
- Promptless-generated translations
- Screenshot updates via Promptless Capture
- Advanced workflow automation controls
- CTA: Book demo

## Enterprise

For teams with massive docs sites or advanced governance needs.

- Price: Custom
- Usage: Unlimited Pages
- Everything in Growth, plus
- Custom integrations
- Audit logs and governance controls
- Data retention controls
- Secure connectivity options
- Dedicated onboarding support
- CTA: Book demo

\* Pages are normalized. A long API reference counts as multiple pages, while short changelog entries may share one.
