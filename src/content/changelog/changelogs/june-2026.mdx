**Improvements:**

* **Sidebar doc collection counts:** The **Suggestions** entry in the sidebar now expands to show each doc collection with a badge counting open suggestions (Ready or PR Open status). Click a collection to jump to the Suggestions page filtered to that collection while preserving your current status filter. All active collections appear, including those with zero open suggestions.
